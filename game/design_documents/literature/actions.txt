M)ENU - Allows the player to access a menu to choose a new game, quit the game, or change the settings.

A)TTACK - This is only enable during combat or when talking to an npc.

C)AST - This allows the player to cast magic spells. They can only cast attack spells during combat or to initiate combat.

I)NVENTORY - This allows the player to manage their inventory and to view their inventory.

S)AVE - This allows players to save their progress in the game.

UP ARROW - Moves the player character up a block

RIGHT ARROW - Moves the player character right a block

DOWN ARROW - Moves the player character down a block

LEFT ARROW - Moves the player character left a block

Be on a block next to NPC and press Q to talk to them.
